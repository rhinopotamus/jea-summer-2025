Our pedagogical approach

  • Thinking about when a solution is "good enough" reinforces a key message of this text, which is, "I'd rather be approximately right than precisely wrong."
  • This common task supports students' development of quantitative reasoning: attending to the things in a story that can be assigned a numerical value, rather than attending solely to the numbers themselves.
