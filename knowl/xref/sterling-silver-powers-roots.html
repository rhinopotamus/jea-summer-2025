Exercise 8.

A set of sterling silverware was valued at $800 in 1920, and the value increased by around 3% per year thereafter.
(Story also appears in 5.1 and 5.1.5)
(a)
What was the value in 1921? Remember, to find 3% of a number we multiply by \(3 \div 100 = 0.03\text{.}\)
(b)
What was the value in 1922? Don’t forget to use your answer to part (a) to calculate the new increase in value.
(c)
