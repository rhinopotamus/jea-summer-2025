Worksheet Exercise 4.

My savings account earns a modest amount of interest, the equivalent of 0.75% annually. I have $12,392.18 in the account now. (Story also appears in A.3.4)

(a)

Name the variables, including units and dependence.

(b)

How much interest will I earn this year? What will my balance be at the end of the year?

(c)

What will my balance be in three years, assuming I neither deposit nor withdraw money?

(d)

Write an equation relating the variables.

(e)

What would the equation be if I moved all of my money into a certificate of deposit earning the equivalent of 0.92%?

(f)

What would the equation be if I moved $10,000 into that certificate of deposit, and kept the rest in savings? Hint: to find the total balance, add the amounts.
