Worksheet Exercise 1.

The size of a round pizza is described by its diameter (distance across). Assuming a 16-inch diameter pizza serves four people, and with a little geometry to help us out, we calculated that a pizza of diameter \(D\) inches serves \(P\) people, where
\begin{equation*} P = 0.015625\ D^2 \end{equation*}
(Story also appears in A.6.2, 4.1.3.c, and 3.3.1)

(a)

Confirm that a 16-inch pizza serves four people.

(b)

How many people does a 12-inch pizza serve? How about a 14-inch pizza?

(c)

Graph the function. Include what happens when \(D=0\text{.}\)

(d)

A personal pizza is sized to serve one person. Use successive approximation to estimate the diameter of a personal pizza to the nearest inch.

(e)

What diameter should an extra large pizza be to serve 6 people? Answer to the nearest inch.
